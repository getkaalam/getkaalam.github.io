We are Kaalam.

Kaalam is a dynamic calendar optimized for the busy life of students. Beta launch planned Fall 2019.

Here's what Kaalam does:

Take in Fixed Events

User inputs commitments, fun events, tasks, and goals

Find Time for Tasks

Kalaam outputs a schedule maximizing user's well-being

Reschedule as Needed

Kaalam automatically updates calendar as user's tasks and events change
